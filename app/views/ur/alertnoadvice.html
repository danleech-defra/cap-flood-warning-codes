River Coln and its tributaries

Latest information

Updated 10:34am Saturday 18 April 2020

Property flooding is not currently expected. River levels are high but steady and within banks on the River Coln as a result of raised groundwater levels. The risk of flooding remains, especially in the Fairford area. The rain we saw overnight has now mostly cleared away to the North although we could see some heavy showers through the day. Tomorrow (Sunday) is expected to be dry. We are closely monitoring the situation. Please remain safe and aware of your local surroundings and refer to the 'River and Sea levels in England' webpage for current river levels. This message will be updated on 19/04/20, or as the situation changes.

Flood risk area: River Coln and its tributaries from Whittington to the confluence with The River Thames

Other ways to check your current flood risk
